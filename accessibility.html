Accessibility

Accessibility statement for Intelligent Manipulation Lab

We want everyone who visits the Intelligent Manipulation Lab website to feel welcome and find the experience rewarding, going above and beyond The Public Sector Bodies Accessibility Regulations 2018.

What are we doing?

To help us make the Intelligent Manipulation Lab website a positive place for everyone, we've been using the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and user friendly for everyone.
The guidelines have three levels of accessibility (A, AA and AAA). Weâ€™ve chosen Level AA as the target for the Intelligent Manipulation Lab website.

How are we doing?

We've worked hard on the Intelligent Manipulation Lab website and believe we've achieved our goal of Level AA accessibility. We monitor the website regularly to maintain this, but if you do find any problems, or if you enjoyed using the Intelligent Manipulation Lab website, please get in touch.
